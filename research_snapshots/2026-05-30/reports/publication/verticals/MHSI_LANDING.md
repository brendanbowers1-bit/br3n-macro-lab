# Metastable Hydride Superconductor Initiative

**Short name:** MHSI · **Materials · Speculative hydride research**

Part of [BR3N Macro Labs](../index.html) · Prepared by Brendan Bowers

---

## Mission

Explore whether metastable hydrogen-rich hydride phases can be computationally identified and prioritized for expert experimental validation through non-equilibrium hydrogen insertion, vacancy filling, pressure-quench chemistry, and safety-first experimental roadmapping.

---

## Flagship Proof-of-Platform

**Mg₂IrH₅ → Mg₂IrH₆**

The initial proof-of-platform concept focuses on whether Mg₂IrH₅ can be evaluated as a hydrogen-deficient parent phase, with Mg₂IrH₆ treated as a **hypothetical metastable hydrogen-inserted target** — not a verified superconducting material.

---

## Core Research Question

Can non-equilibrium hydrogen insertion or vacancy filling create metastable hydride phases that are electronically, dynamically, and chemically plausible enough to justify deeper computational and experimental study?

## Scientific Disclaimer

This initiative is **speculative research planning**. It does not claim discovery of a superconductor, does not claim verified room-temperature superconductivity, and does not claim that Mg₂IrH₆ has been experimentally synthesized or validated.

All candidate phases require computational validation, experimental feasibility review, safety review, and independent replication.

BR3N Macro Labs is an independent research project — not affiliated with any university, laboratory, or research institution unless explicitly stated.
