# Limitations

## Data limitations

- Demo/mock data present until Tier 1–2 official files are ingested
- Reserve scores may lag attestations; not real-time liquidity
- Off-ramp and compliance delays rely on manual placeholders in corridors
- Usage proxies for dollarization are coarse; reverse causality possible
- Chain finality assumptions vary by network and congestion regime

## Method limitations

- Indices are associations under stated weights — not causal welfare estimates
- Sensitivity cases change assumptions; rankings may shift materially
- DRV describes run **conditions** — does not predict runs
- SVSI does not assume stablecoins are cheaper; measures survival under frictions
- Bivariate hypothesis tests omit full control sets

## Scope limitations

- Not financial advice, investment recommendation, or operational treasury guidance
- Not legal opinion on finality or enforceability
- Not a trading or portfolio tool

See `LAB_LIMITATIONS` in `src/config/research_settings.py`.
