# Target Labs and Collaboration Map

## Purpose

Identify academic, national laboratory, and industry groups that may be relevant for computational hydride screening, high-pressure synthesis, hydrogen insertion chemistry, and superconductivity characterization.

## Lab Categories

### Computational Materials Groups

**Relevant expertise:**

- DFT
- phonon calculations
- electron-phonon coupling
- metastability
- hydride screening

### High-Pressure Hydride Labs

**Relevant expertise:**

- diamond anvil cells
- laser heating
- high-pressure hydrogenation
- pressure-quench attempts
- hydride superconductivity

### Solid-State Chemistry Labs

**Relevant expertise:**

- topochemical reactions
- hydride synthesis
- hydrogen insertion
- metastable phases
- powder diffraction

### Superconductivity Measurement Labs

**Relevant expertise:**

- transport
- magnetization
- heat capacity
- low-temperature measurements
- high-pressure measurements

### National Labs

**Relevant expertise:**

- neutron diffraction
- high-pressure beamlines
- materials characterization
- advanced computation

## Outreach Criteria

Prioritize labs that:

- publish on hydrides or superconductivity,
- have high-pressure or hydrogen expertise,
- can evaluate safety,
- can perform structural characterization,
- are open to collaboration,
- have computational screening experience.

## Target Lab Record Template

For each lab:

- lab name
- institution
- principal investigator
- area of expertise
- relevant papers
- why relevant
- contact status
- outreach date
- response
- next step
