# Bowers Frontier Macro Labs — Brand Guide

## Brand name

**Bowers Frontier Macro Labs**

## Short name

**Bowers Frontier**

## Tagline options

- AI-native macro intelligence for FX, treasury, and cross-border settlement.
- Frontier research systems for global macro and corridor risk.
- Domain-specific AI for FX, treasury, payments, and settlement intelligence.

## Positioning

Bowers Frontier Macro Labs builds domain-specific AI systems for FX, treasury, settlement, cross-border payments, corridor risk, and macro-financial intelligence.

**Primary first product:** USD/MXN Corridor Intelligence System.

## Voice

Serious, institutional, research-driven, precise, evidence-based, non-hype.

## Avoid

- Get-rich language
- Guaranteed trading claims
- Fake performance claims
- Crypto hype
- Overuse of “Nobel”
- Overuse of “insane”
- Toy chatbot positioning

## Preferred phrases

- Corridor intelligence
- Settlement risk
- Treasury decision support
- Model-grounded research
- Data quality standards
- Transparent scoring
- Financial market infrastructure intelligence
- Cross-border payment intelligence

## Homepage copy (Macro Lab)

**Hero:** Bowers Frontier Macro Labs

**Subtext:** Domain-specific AI for FX, treasury, settlement, and cross-border payment intelligence.

**Supporting copy:** We build research systems that combine market data, macro signals, corridor analytics, model evaluation, and AI-assisted reporting to study global financial flows with institutional discipline.

Implemented in `macro-lab.html` via `src/site_builder.py` and constants in `src/__init__.py`.

## Visual identity

- Institutional, frontier research, macro intelligence
- Black/white base, restrained accent colors
- Credible for treasury, fintech, FX, AI, and macro researchers
- Use **Bowers Frontier institutional macro aesthetic** for design language

## Parent institute

**Bowers Frontier Institute** — parent research institute. Bowers Frontier Macro Labs is the operational macro division and integrated platform.
