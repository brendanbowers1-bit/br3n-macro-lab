## Treasury perspective

Settlement operations require: cutoffs, prefunding, liquidity buffers, FX exposure during settlement windows, reconciliation, and failure management. Each creates measurable economic drag.

## Model specifications

Each flagship model ships in **core**, **risk-adjusted**, and/or **extended** variants where applicable. Extended components are labeled and excluded from baseline inference.

## Identification

All empirical tests report associations under stated specifications. Causal claims require instruments or natural experiments not yet implemented in Stage 1.
